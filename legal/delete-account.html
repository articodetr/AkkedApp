Account Deletion Request - Akked App

Last updated: June 3, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your account and associated data for the Akked Android app, package name com.articode.akked.

You can delete your account directly from the app settings screen, or send a deletion request by email:
jallalalomary@gmail.com

How to request deletion by email

Please send an email to the address above and include:

  • Email subject: Akked Account Deletion Request
  • The email address used for your Akked account.
  • A written confirmation that you want your account and associated data deleted.

Data that will be deleted

  • Your user account and related authentication data.
  • Your customer records, financial movements, and balances.
  • Your shop settings, logo, and document letterhead linked to the account.
  • Notifications and account activity data linked to the app account.

Data that may be retained

We may retain limited records if required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention reasons. If mandatory retention applies, we will explain it when processing the request.

Processing time

Account deletion requests are reviewed within a reasonable period. We may ask you to confirm ownership of the email address before deletion to protect your account from unauthorized requests.

Account deletion is final and may remove access to your data inside the app.
